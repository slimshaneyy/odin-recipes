Cripsy Garlic Green Beans

Super crispy, salty and garlicky green beans. These will make your mouth water

Ingredients:

  • Trimmed and washed green beans
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Freshly chopped or minced garlic

Steps:

  1. Heat skillet to medium-high heat
  2. Place desired amount of olive oil in pan
  3. Put green beans in skillet, season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. At about halfway to desired doneness, throw in garlic to taste
  5. Remove from heat whenever green beans are to your liking
