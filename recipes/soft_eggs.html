Soft-Boiled Eggs

Beautifully runny and easy to make soft-boiled eggs. Perfect for ramen, avocado toast, eggs-benedict, or anything else.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Bring Saucepan to boil with enough water to cover single layer of eggs
  2. Put eggs fresh from refrigerator into boiling water
  3. Cook for exactly 6 1/2 minutes
  4. Remove with slotted spoon and place in icewater until cool to touch
